Our Services

Admission to the UniCrew unicycle school includes lessons four times a month. Unicycles, helmets, pads, and other safety gear will be provided during classes and included in tuition. Insurance is also provided for free.

What's in a lesson?

Each lesson is individualized for each student depending on skill level and prior experience. Basic lessons for beginners include learning to mount, learning to ride with a buddy, and eventually learning to ride solo. More advanced lessons might include learning to ride backwards, jump, idling, wheel walking, and other tricks. Riders will be separated by skill levels 1-10, which can be found here.

Prices

The UniCrew will offer different payment plans for class sessions. (One month includes 4 two hour lessons).

  • $50 per month
  • $250 per 6 months
  • $450 per year
