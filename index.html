The Unicrew

-Unicycle School in Bakersfield-

Who we are...

The UniCrew is a unicycle school based in Bakersfield, California. Our business was founded in 2016 by Justin Rappleye and Anthony Limiero, experienced world-class unicyclists with nearly three and a half years experience.

What we do...

Extreme unicycling is a pretty cool sport. It isn't just simply riding. It includes obstacles, tricks, and skatepark like elements. We teach everything from basic riding to advanced tricks.

What we've done...

What started as a fun hobby turned into much more. We have taken the sport of unicycling to great lengths, through performances at local schools and competing internationally in San Sebastian, Spain, and now, The UniCrew unicycle school.
