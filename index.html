The Unicrew

-Extreme unicyclists from Bakersfield-

Who we are...

The Unicrew is a group of extreme unicyclists based in Bakersfield, California. The group was founded by Jason Colaw and Anthony Limiero in 2013, and now includes over 20 members worldwide, including members in D.C., Oklahoma, Tennessee, Colorado, Paraguay, Haiti, and more.

What we do...

Extreme unicycling is a pretty cool sport. It isn't just simply riding. It includes obstacles, tricks, and skatepark like elements.

More...

What we've done...

What started as a fun hobby turned into much more. We have taken the sport of unicycling to great lengths, through performances at local schools and competing internationally in San Sebastian, Spain.
